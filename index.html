What is Aqourainbow?

In Yuuki Wa Doko Ni? Kimi No Mune Ni! the verse before Chika's solo, please hide your penlight by tucking under your arm, or lowering it behind your seat in front of you.The theater should turn completely dark during this time.

Aaah, atsuku naru imi ga wakari katete

While your penlight is hidden, swap to the assigned color, then wait your cue to be sung.

Bring up your penlight with the first syllable of your lyrics. Continbue raising your penlight for the rest of the solo.

Kokoro ga motomeru hokorashisa Hashiri tsudzukete tsukameru mirai Yume ga(takusam) Yume ga(takusan) Kienai(Yume ga)

After the rainbow is formed, please keep your assigned color for the remainder of the song!

What is Kananrail?

Kananrail takes place during Happy Party Train. The goal is to emulate the shining railroad tracks in the animated music video.

Seats that are part of the railroad tracks are normally the left and right sections of the theater.

What is Yousoroad?

Yousoroad takes place during You Watanabe’s solo in Koi ni Naritai AQUARIUM. The goal is to re-create the shining blue road in the animated music video.

Seats that are part of the blue light road are the middle strip of the theater

What is No.10  Rainbow?

In No.10, before Aqours starts counting,please hide your penlight by tucking under your arm, or lowering it behind your seat in front of you.The theater should turn completely dark during this time.

While your penlight is hidden, swap to the assigned color, then wait your cue to be sung.

Ichi,Ni,San,
Yon,Go,Roku,
Nana,Hachi,Jyuu
Juu!

Bring up your penlight with the count of your assigned member. Please keep your penlight up for the remainder of the song!
